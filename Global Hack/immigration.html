Foreign Born Guidance

Our Mission

To give immigrants the knowledge they need to feel comfortable living in the U.S

Our Vision

We want to see all immigrants that go through our program be able to function comfortably in U.S society.

What We Do

We provide courses to immigrants to help them assimilate to normal life in the United States. Those courses will provide neccesary knowledge to know what to do in everyday situations, such as interviewing for a job, making a resume, or when you are pulled over.

Courses

Resume Job Interview
Employment is hard to get into in the U.S, and that's why these courses help. These courses are designed to help you get into the application process and get employed in your selected field, and how you can become comfortable in the workplace.
Credit Score Debt Budgeting Taxes
Finances are crazy in the U.S and these courses can guide you to better personal finance. From budgeting to credit score to how to read the bills in your mail these courses help you understand finances in the U.S.
Basic Schooling Advanced Schooling Non-English Schooling
Schooling is very important in the U.S. These courses help guide you towards schools that fit your needs, what happens at school, and what you need to do after you have completed schooling.
