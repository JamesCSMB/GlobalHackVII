Basic Schooling

    Levels of schooling

  • Elementary

    In the U.S. Elementary School goes through the grades PreK-5 with average ages being 5-11.

  • Jr High

    In the U.S. Jr High goes through the grades 6-8 with average ages 11-14. After this you move on to High School.

  • High School

    In the U.S. High School goes through the grades 9-12 (Freshman, Sophomore, Junior, Senior) with average ages 14-18. After this you move on to college or a trade school.

Need

Education is one of the biggest needs in the U.S. Education is the key to getting a good job. It is the best way for kids to be introduced into American culture and to learn how to interact with others through schooling.
