Resume

    Type

  • Functional

    Good option for people who have gaps in their professional experience. The functional resume highlights skills and abilities, as opposed to the chronological order of experience

  • Chronological

    A chronological resume is one of the most common resume formats and there's a good reason for it: Employers tend to prefer it because it's straightforward and easy to scan

  • Combination

    Marries the best aspects of a chronological resume and functional resume it uses both work history and skills to capture the employer's attention.

Need

Resumes are what jobs look for when you apply to get to a job. They look at this to see what type of person you are before they meet you.
